About

I am now a lecture at the department of BioMedical Engineering (BME), FMMU. I was a Ph.D student in the Advanced Radar Signal Processing (ARSP) group of NUDT, where I worked with Dr. Hongqi Fan in Adavance Radar Signal Processing (ARSP) group. My research interests lie broadly on biomedical signal processing, optimization, tracking and estimation.

Academia

My primary research is structural information assisted Compressive Sensing (CS) and optimisation methods. I apply these algorithms on Direction-of-Arrival (DOA) estimation, radar imaging and tracking. We (in collaborate with Dr. Zhilin Zhang) also treated CS and Quantized CS as a low-complexity, low-power, robust data compression method for bio-signals (ECG, EEG, PPG, etc.) telemonitoring applications. In Dept. of Biomedical Enegineering, FMMU, I am now building an advanced, yet small-form-factor Electrical Impedance Tomograpgy (EIT) system.
