About

I am a lecture at the department of BioMedical Engineering (BME), FMMU. My research interests are structural information assisted Compressive Sensing (CS) and optimisation methods. We (in collaborate with Dr. Zhilin Zhang) applied CS and Quantized CS as a low-complexity, low-power, robust data compression method for bio-signals (ECG, EEG, PPG, etc.) telemonitoring applications. In Dept. of Biomedical Enegineering, FMMU, I develop advanced, small-form-factor Electrical Impedance Tomograpgy (EIT) technologies.
