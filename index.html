About

I am now a research assistant in BME at FMMU, under the supervisor of Prof. XiuZhen Dong and Prof. Feng Fu. I am also a fifth-year Ph.D in ATR Lab at NUDT, where I worked with Prof. Qiang Fu and Dr. Hongqi Fan in Adavance Radar Signal Processing (ARSP) group. My research interests lie broadly on biomedical signal processing, statistics, optimization, tracking and estimation.

Academia

My primary research focuses on developing advanced, side-information assisted Compressive Sensing (CS) algorithms, utilizing the Bayesian Inference method. These CS algorithms are then used in group target Direction-of-Arrival (DOA) estimation and dynamic scene tracking. In corporate with Dr. Zhilin Zhang, we apply CS as a low complexity, low-power, robust data compression method in wireless bio-signals (ECG, EMG, EEG, etc.) telemonitoring applications, a.k.a, Portable Healthcare (you may also call it eHealth or mHealth, however portable healthcare is IMHO future).

I am now building Electrical Impedance Tomograpgy (EIT) systems at Dept. Biomedical Enegineering, FMMU under the supervisor of Prof. XiuZhen Dong and Prof. Feng Fu, and researching related imaging techiniques and hardware platforms. I am also a big electronic fan on amatuer radio (Cadsoft Eagle, HackRF, GNURadio), embedded platforms (ARM cortex-M series, Xilinx FPGAs, and Labview) and Bluetooth technology (Nordic Chips, nrf51822).

An amatuer RADAR utilizing the FMCW waveform is building on the way, with Dr. Xia Yang, Dr. Hongqi Fan and Prof. Zaiqi Lu.
