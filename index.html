Academia

My primary research is side-information assisted Compressive Sensing (CS). We (in collaborate with Dr. Zhilin Zhang) have developed advanced Bayesian learning algorithms  —  the BSBL framework. I apply these algorithms on Direction-of-Arrival (DOA) estimation, radar imaging and tracking. We also applied CS as a low-complexity, low-power, robust data compression method in bio-signals (ECG, EEG, PPG, etc.) telemonitoring applications.

My current hack-a-project is building advanced and yet small-form-factor Electrical Impedance Tomograpgy (EIT) prototyping hardware at Dept. of Biomedical Enegineering, FMMU. And yes, CS technology plays a important role in data acquisition !

I am also a big electronic fan in amatuer radio (HackRF, GNURadio), homebrew PCB (Kicad, Cadsoft Eagle), embedded platforms (ARM cortex-M series, Xilinx FPGAs, and Labview) and Bluetooth technology (Roving Networks RN-42, Nordic nrf51822). An novel DCR (Detect and Communication enabled RADAR) is building on the way, with Dr. Hongqi Fan.
